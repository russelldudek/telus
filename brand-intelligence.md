## Typography evidence and decision

The official TELUS design system identifies Helvetica Neue as the primary typeface and describes a precise, friendly, neutral typographic character. No proprietary font binaries are copied or committed. The campaign uses the licensed/system stack: Helvetica Neue, Helvetica, Arial, Liberation Sans, sans-serif. Light large headings, stronger small text, modular sizing, readable line length and generous leading reproduce the documented behavior.

## Photography and illustration character

No generic stock photography is used. Employer recognition comes from the official wordmark, official color system, clean white space, precise typography and restrained green motion. Candidate-original visuals translate the role into a CX AI Workshare Map made of interaction cells and layered decision planes.

## Geometry and interface motifs

- White-dominant surfaces with TELUS purple hierarchy and green activation cues.
- Rounded but not toy-like containers.
- Clean ruled dividers and generous white space.
- Organic diagonal authority boundary inspired by the TELUS motion mark without copying or animating the logo.
- Modular interaction cells rather than generic cards or a dashboard grid.

## Motion character

The hero interaction cells change state when a user selects a portfolio scenario. The transition explains a shift among Human-led, Assistive, Conversational and Agentic work. No official mark is animated. Reduced-motion mode resolves every state immediately.

## Recommended use by artifact

- Candidate vision: full brand system and interactive Workshare Map.
- Resume and cover letter: restrained logo, purple rule, green section labels and clean white page.
- Interview brief and 120-day plan: same header, scorecard/table grammar and evidence-based typography.
- Portfolio investment memo: landscape decision sheet with the same official identity and workshare language.

## Independent-candidate distinction

All surfaces state that the work is an independent candidate product by Russell Dudek. TELUS Digital trademarks are used only to identify the target employer; no endorsement is implied.
