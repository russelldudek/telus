# TELUS Digital — Managing Director, CX AI Delivery

Independent candidate campaign by Russell Dudek.

## Candidate thesis

**Redesign the workshare. Compound the delivery advantage.**

The campaign argues that TELUS Digital's real opportunity is to convert global CX operator scale, AI capability, platform partnerships and frontline learning into a repeatable delivery practice that grows revenue, protects margins and earns adoption.

## Campaign experience

- Candidate vision and interactive CX AI Workshare Map
- Exactly two-page printable resume
- Exactly one-page printable cover letter
- Four-page interview thesis brief
- Two-page 120-day entry plan
- One-page landscape CX AI Portfolio Investment Memo
- Hard-objection analysis and executive interview questions

The web experience reconstructs a checksum-verified candidate-facing source package in the browser and exposes each artifact as a printable view. The separately delivered campaign archive contains the validated PDF set.
