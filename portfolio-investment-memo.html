CX AI Portfolio Investment Memo

Candidate-created one-page review for a managed account, new offer or transformation opportunity. This is not a TELUS Digital internal process.

1. Client outcome and sponsor

What result matters? Who owns it? What current evidence shows the problem is material?

2. Interaction portfolio

Which intents, volumes, complexity levels, failure modes and customer moments are in scope?

3. Commercial shape

Managed-account expansion, transformation project, outcomes-based pilot, partner-led offer or another model?

4. Workshare decision

What remains Human-led? What becomes Assistive, Conversational or Agentic?

5. Human authority boundary

Where are approval, exception, escalation, judgment and accountability mandatory?

6. Platform and partner composition

Existing CCaaS/CRM, TELUS Digital IP, partner technology, data, knowledge and integration path.

7. Evidence to earn scale

Client outcome, adoption, service quality, unit economics, delivery burden, risk and trust.

8. Change burden and ownership

Who must work differently? Who owns training, operations, exceptions, production and continuous improvement?

9. Reuse thesis

Client-specific solution, vertical pattern, partner play, core offer module or reusable operating mechanism?

SEQUENCE
resolve readiness or evidence first

STOP
return capacity to higher-value work
